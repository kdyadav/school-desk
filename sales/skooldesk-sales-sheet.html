Why skoolDesk

The problem. Most school ERPs take months to deploy, lock data inside a vendor's database, fall over when the internet does, and force every persona — admin, teacher, parent — through the same clumsy interface.

Our answer. A self-contained, offline-first platform that brands itself to your school in minutes, gives every role its own tailored experience, and keeps your data on your devices — under your control.

20 capabilities, mapped to outcomes

1 Zero-Setup Onboarding

Live in 10 minutes, not 10 weeks.

  • Two-step setup wizard — school details + admin account, and you're operational.
  • Self-branding at runtime — name, tagline, contact info, social links, brand colour, logo, favicon — all configured by the school.
  • Live theming — document title, browser tab icon, and theme colour update instantly across the app.
  • Editable any time — admin revisits School Profile to update branding without raising a ticket.
Pitch: "Your school's logo and colours, on your school's URL, by tomorrow morning."

2 Multi-Tenant by Design

Your data is yours, period.

  • Per-deployment tenancy — each install is its own world; no shared database with other schools.
  • Local-first storage — student records, fees, salaries live on the school's own device.
  • No vendor lock-in — data is always accessible to the school, never held hostage.
Pitch: "Other vendors put 500 schools in one database. We give you your own."

3 Role-Based Access for the Whole Community

One platform, four audiences, four tailored experiences.

  • Admin — full control of academics, people, fees, payroll, exams, reports.
  • Teacher — only their classes, timetable, attendance, marks, payslips.
  • Student — own timetable, attendance, results, report cards, fee status.
  • Parent / Guardian — ward's attendance, marks, dues, parent announcements.
  • bcrypt password hashing — same algorithm banks use; no plain text passwords.
  • Route-level role guards — wrong-role pages are blocked at the navigation layer.
Pitch: "The right person sees the right thing — and never anything more."

4 Academic Setup Done Right

Model your school exactly as it operates.

  • Academic years — one active year drives defaults across the system.
  • Classes & sections — multiple sections per class (V-A, V-B…), each with its class teacher.
  • Subjects — code-based master (MATH-9, SCI-10) reused across classes.
  • Class teacher assignment — auto-surfaced in attendance and reports.
Pitch: "Set it up once at the start of the year — the rest of the system inherits it."

5 People Management — One Source of Truth

Stop juggling Excel sheets for students, staff, and parents.

  • Students — admission number (unique), name, current section, linked guardian.
  • Guardians — separate entity; one parent of three students = one record.
  • Teachers & staff — employee number, official email, full profile.
  • User accounts — every login is linked to a real person record. No duplicates.
  • Database-enforced uniqueness on admission numbers, employee numbers, emails.
Pitch: "One student, one record, one truth — across attendance, fees, exams, and report cards."
